Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

Bajo (the "Studio") develops games and tools focused on privacy and user experience. This policy explains our practices across all our digital properties, including our website and mobile applications.

1. Data Collection

Our current portfolio, including Proxima 8, is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers. All gameplay data and progress are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

We use standard distribution platforms which may collect basic usage data:

  • Google Play Services: For app delivery, security, and basic distribution metrics.
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting our static web content.

3. Children’s Privacy

Our products are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

4. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us via our official support channels.
